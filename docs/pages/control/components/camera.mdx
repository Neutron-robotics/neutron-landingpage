### Camera Controller

![Camera Controller Screenshot](/screenshot/component-camera.png)

**Required Controllers:**
* [Camera Controller](/neutron-graph/controllers/camera-controller)
* [Camera Frame](/neutron-graph/controllers/camera-frame) 

The Camera Controller component is a powerful tool that enables seamless integration of camera functionality into your web application.
You can use the Camera Controller to dynamically turn on the remote camera, and visualize the video stream in real time with low latency
