## Create an Account

The first step is to create an account on the Neutron Robotic platform. Visit our [registration page](https://neutron-robotics.com/register) and fill in the required details. Once registered, you will receive a confirmation email with a link to activate your account.

You can find informations related to your account in the settings menu. You can update your personal information such as first name, last name, email, and profile picture informations
