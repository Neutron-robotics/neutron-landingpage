## Step 2: Create a Team

After activating your account, log in to the platform and navigate to the "Organization" section. You are by default not member of an team,
you will need to create a team.

Feel free to enter details about it such as the project name,
description, and any initial configurations. This will help you organize your work and set the foundation for your robotic solutions.
