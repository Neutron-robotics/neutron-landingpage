# Where do I begin ?

### Step 1: Create an Account

The first step is to create an account on the Neutron Robotic platform. Visit our [registration page](https://neutronrobotic.com/register) and fill in the required details. Once registered, you will receive a confirmation email with a link to activate your account.

### Step 2: Set Up Your First Project

After activating your account, log in to the platform and navigate to the "Organization" section. You are by default not member of an team, you will need to [create your first team](https://neutronrobotic.com/create-team). Feel free to enter details about it such as the project name, description, and any initial configurations. This will help you organize your work and set the foundation for your robotic solutions.

### Step 3: Define Your Robotic Systems

Within your project, you can start defining your robotic systems. Go to the "Robotic Systems" tab and click on "Add New System." Here, you can specify the type of robots you are using, their configurations, and any specific parameters required for their operation. Our platform provide native support for **ROS2** defined systems but is perfectly able to support all type of robotics systems.
